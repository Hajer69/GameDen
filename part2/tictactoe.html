Tic Tac Toe

A classic strategy game for two players who take turns marking X and O in a 3x3 grid.

Strategy Easy 2 Players
Game Rules & Information
Objective Be the first to get three of your marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
Players 2 players (X and O)
Skills Developed Strategic thinking, Pattern recognition, Planning
Game Board 3x3 grid
Average Game Time 1-3 minutes
Strategy Level Tips Difficulty
Beginner Try to get three in a row, block opponent Easy
Intermediate Create forks (two ways to win), control center Medium
Expert Force opponent moves, set up multiple threats Hard

Features

  • Play against friend or computer AI
  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Win/loss statistics tracking
  • Learn advanced strategies
