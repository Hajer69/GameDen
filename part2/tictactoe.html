Tic Tac Toe

A classic strategy game for two players who take turns marking X and O in a 3x3 grid.

Game Rules & Information

Objective Be the first to get three of your marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
Players 1 player vs Computer or 2 players
Skills Developed Strategic thinking, Pattern recognition, Planning
Game Features 3 Difficulty levels, Score tracking, Smart AI
Average Game Time 1-3 minutes

Difficulty Levels

Difficulty Computer Strategy Win Rate Challenge Level
Easy Random moves 20% chance Beginner
Medium Basic defense and offense 50% chance Intermediate
Hard Advanced minimax algorithm 90% chance Expert
