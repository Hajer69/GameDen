Rock Paper Scissors

A classic hand game that tests your prediction skills and strategy against the computer.

Game Rules & Information

How to Play Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The computer will also choose.
Winning Rules Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beat Paper, Paper beats Rock
Skills Developed Strategic thinking, Pattern recognition, Quick decision making
Game Features 3 Difficulty levels, Score tracking, Countdown animation
Average Game Time 2-5 minutes per game
